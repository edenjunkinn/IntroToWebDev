Calgary, Alberta

Location

Calgary is located in the province of Alberta in Canada.

About

Fact 1

As of 2021, the city had a population of 1,306,784 and a metropolitan population of 1,481,806

Fact 2

Calgary's economy includes activity in the energy, financial services, film and television, transportation and logistics, technology, manufacturing, aerospace, health and wellness, retail, and tourism sectors.

Fact 3

According to the 2016 Census, 60% of Calgary's population was of European origin
